Tool 05
Elevator Pitch Trainer
Part 1 · Who you are (10 sec)
I'm Vladimiras — a Lead DevOps Engineer with 18 years building mission-critical infrastructure in fintech, banking, and regulated government environments.
State name + title + industry. No fluff. One sentence.
Part 2 · What you do (20 sec)
I design and operate production Kubernetes platforms, GitOps pipelines, and compliance automation systems — the infrastructure that keeps regulated services running at scale. Most recently I built the EU payment compliance reporting system at Walletto from scratch and ran national-scale data platforms for government clients in Kazakhstan.
2-3 concrete things. Always with context — regulated, national-scale, from scratch.
Part 3 · What makes you different (20 sec)
What sets me apart is end-to-end ownership — I don't just maintain platforms, I architect them, build the runbooks, debug the 2am incidents, and hand them off properly. I'm also running production AI infrastructure on my own K3s cluster: LLM inference, MCP server integrations, GPU scheduling. That's not a line on a CV — that's operational experience before it became a requirement.
Your real differentiator. Not soft skills — evidence of depth.
Part 4 · What you want (10 sec)
I'm looking for a senior platform or DevOps lead role where the hard problems are still ahead — where I can own architecture decisions, not just execute someone else's plan.
One clear sentence. What kind of role, what kind of challenge.

Edit your pitch below. Aim for 120–150 words — that's roughly 60 seconds spoken at a natural pace.

Quick tips
⏱️
90-second rule
STAR answers should be 90–120 sec. Longer kills attention. Shorter loses impact.
🎯
Customize one answer
"Why this role?" must be unique per company. All others can be standard.
📊
Numbers win
Every answer needs at least one measurable result. "Faster" is weak. "4x faster" lands.
🔁
Daily practice
10 min vocabulary + 1 mock run per day. Muscle memory beats last-minute cramming.
